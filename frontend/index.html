The Sheriff's Last Conversation

New Mexico, 1881. A dim lamp flickers in a dark room. On the desk — a badge and a revolver. In the corner, the wounded sheriff lies in bed.

Inspired by Bob Dylan's "Knockin' on Heaven's Door"

The sheriff watches you enter...

The lamp goes out.

A door knocks somewhere far away...

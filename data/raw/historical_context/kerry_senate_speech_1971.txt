John Kerry — Vietnam Veterans Against the War Statement
U.S. Senate Committee on Foreign Relations, Washington, D.C., April 23, 1971

Context: Vietnam Gazileri adına savaşın anlamsızlığını anlatırken madalyalarını fırlatıp attıkları dönemin en meşhur konuşması. Şerifin rozetini bırakmasıyla birebir örtüşür.

"We are asking Americans to think about that because how do you ask a man to be the last man to die in Vietnam? How do you ask a man to be the last man to die for a mistake? ... We are also here to ask, and we are here to ask vehemently, where are the leaders of our country? ... We found that not only was it a civil war, an effort by a people who had for years been seeking their liberation from any colonial influence whatsoever, but also we found that the men whom we had, in fact, shaped from our own stamp, the men in Saigon, were a corrupt, dictatorial regime."
